    Women in Engineering

    “I believe in the unlimited power of women in the context of science and engineering”

    -Elizabeth Holmes

    Women In Engineering is a IEEE Afilliate Group, dedicated to changing the face of engineering by promoting women in STEM & leadership roles, inspiring young girls to achieve their goals in science.
